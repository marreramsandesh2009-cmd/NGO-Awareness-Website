About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a social organization focused on education, women empowerment, animal welfare, and community development. Our mission is to create positive impact through sustainable initiatives and youth participation.

Ongoing Projects

Project Seva

Providing food and clothing to underprivileged communities.

Project Bachpanshala

Supporting education and digital literacy for children.

Project Jeev

Caring for stray animals through feeding and welfare activities.

Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development and awareness programs.

Project Prakriti

Environmental Conservation and Sustainable Efforts.

Project Vikas

Enhancing Employability through skill Development Programs.
